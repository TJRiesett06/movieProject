What happens next?

So now that you have a great diet and nutrition road map what's next?

A solid diet road map is just the start of great diet and nutrition. We recommend writing down or downloading an app that tracks your macros and calories. This will allow you to see how you are doing and stay within your road maps goal boundaries. Along with tracking we also recommend some level of physical activity for all goals! The abundance of free great workouts online makes it easy to test different workouts until you find one you love! Gyms not your thing? ADDITIONAL SUPPORT


Info about the formula:

The Fitness Formula uses a combination of your BMI, goal weight, goal length, and activity level to best create a diet map for you. The formula focuses on what we believe are the three main parameters of any diet; calories, protein, and carbohydrates. Although a diet can take into account many other factors including fat intake, water consumption, or number of meals, we wanted to keep this formula simple, a starting roadmap as we call it, to reaching your fitness and nutrition goals! If you are unhappy with your results, please feel free to redo the formula!
