Different diets to try out!



The Vegetarian diet

People who follow the vegetarian diet do not eat any types of meat or fish. Some health benefits include lower cholesterol levels and increased weight loss vs none-vegetarian diets. The vegetarian diet is also a common diet for people who are conscience about the treatment of animals.




The Vegan diet

The Vegan Diet is similar in many ways to the vegetarian diet, no meat or fish. On top of these vegans do not eat any animal products. This includes cheese, milk, and eggs among many other foods. Another difference between the vegetarian and vegan diets is that vegans do not use animal products of any kinds, the main items being clothing. Some benefits to the vegan diet are increased overall nutrition, and proven heart health benefits!




The Keto diet

The standard keto diet is a diet where 10% or less of a persons calories come from carbohydrates. There are many benefits to the keto diet including lower blood sugar and insulin levels, faster weight loss, and reduced appetite! One downfall to the traditional keto diet is the lack of the macronutrient protein essential for muscle growth; however, if gaining muscle isn't your goal than the keto diet is worth looking into.




The bottom line on diets

Your diet is an extremely important part of your life. It effects your health, mind, and overall well-being. That being said before you choose to change your diet, especially if you are considering one of the special diets above , is it vital to do your own extra research. Diets, like those above have science backed benefits, but they can also lead to harmful unintended consequence. Keep this in mind when using The Fitness Formula's roadmap, and remember everyone is unique, and we all need to be aware of different aspects when it comes to diet.
